Land of mountains, land by the river, Land of fields, land of cathedrals, Land of hammers, with a promising future! Home to great daughters and sons, People highly gifted for beautiful arts, Much-praised Austria! Strongly feuded for, fiercely hard-fought for, Thou liest in the middle of the continent Like a strong heart, Since the early days of the ancestors thou hast Borne the burden of a high mission, Much-tried Austria. Bravely towards the new ages See us striding, free, and faithful, Assiduous and full of hope, Unified, let us in jolly choirs Pledge allegiance to thee, Fatherland Much-beloved Austria.
